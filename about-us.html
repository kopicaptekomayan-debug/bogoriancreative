About Us Welcome to Bogorian Creative. Bogorian Creative is an Indonesia-based workshop specializing in the production of premium display food, food replicas, artificial food, and custom food models for commercial, promotional, and visual merchandising purposes. Located in Cisarua, Bogor, West Java, we serve restaurants, cafés, bakeries, hotels, food courts, franchise businesses, event organizers, educational institutions, and creative agencies throughout Indonesia. Founded and managed by Cecep Komarudin, Bogorian Creative has over six years of experience delivering high-quality custom food replicas through online marketplaces, direct business partnerships, and project-based orders. Our products are designed to help businesses improve visual presentation, strengthen brand image, enhance customer experience, and create attractive product displays without the limitations of real food. Every project is carefully handcrafted using selected materials and detailed finishing techniques to achieve realistic appearance, durability, and long-term display performance. We can produce replicas based on existing menu items, physical samples, technical specifications, or customer reference photographs.
